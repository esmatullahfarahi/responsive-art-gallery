Leonardo Da Vinci, Mona Lisa

Da Vinci's Mona Lisa is mysterious subject, enigmatic smile, captivating landscape.

Creation Date: 1503-1517

Johannes Vermeer, Girl with a Pearl Earring

Johannes Vermeer's painting: lifelike and modern, possibly influenced by a camera obscura.

Creation Date: 1665

Gustav Klimt, The Kiss

Opulently gilded and extravagantly patterned, The Kiss, Gustav Klimt’s fin-de-siècle portrayal of intimacy, is a mix of Symbolism and Vienna Jugendstil.

Creation Date: 1907–1908

Sandro Botticelli, The Birth of Venus

Botticelli’s The Birth of Venus was the first full-length, non-religious nude since antiquity, and was made for Lorenzo de Medici.

Creation Date: 1484–1486

James Abbott McNeill Whistler

Whistler’s Mother, or Arrangement in Grey and Black No. 1, as it’s actually titled, speaks to the artist’s ambition to pursue art for art’s sake.

Creation Date: 1871

Jan van Eyck, The Arnolfini Portrait

One of the most significant works produced during the Northern Renaissance, this composition is believed to be one of the first paintings executed in oils.Description of Art Piece 6.
